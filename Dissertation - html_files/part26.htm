Results from the resnet50_unet model (table 1) with 10 epochs training show a frequency-weighted IOU score of 23.06077815809837%; the trash label had an IOU score of 26.206308%. Both these values are low and therefore imply a low accuracy overall, and the trash label is the most meaningful identification for this project. Looking at figure 11, we can see the predicted pixels are sporadically laid out, with not much in common with the original image. This indicates that most of the pixels in the IOU score are more or less random placements.
