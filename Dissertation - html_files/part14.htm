III: Conclusion

Within the machine learning field, much research has been done into reducing the amount of contamination within recycling. Many approaches have been researched, such as smart bins, robots, production line robots, mobile applications, and more.


Many of the research papers have been insightful into what has worked for them and what could be researched in the future to further the ideas. For example, most research highlighted that image detection on a single object is very effective and accurate. More research into identifying different contaminants in a large amount of recycling will push the field further as it provides more uses within the current infrastructure, e.g., sorting after collection.
